# Direction

Memphis started as a complete music experience.

The interface, player, collections and discovery logic proved the idea. They also exposed the cost of depending on audio, artwork and catalog data owned by other platforms.

We are keeping the innovation, not rebuilding what already exists.

## Next version

- Listening happens anywhere.
- Memphis starts with the experience around it.
- Context, feeling, habits and reflection become the data.
- External music references remain optional.
- Audio and third-party artwork are not required.

## What stays

Everything.

The music interface remains as a design archive: songs, albums, artists, playlists, player, discovery and the components connecting them.

It documents what was built and becomes a foundation for the next version.
